About us
MSU-IIT Langkit University Shop
Welcome to Langkit, the official merchandise store of Mindanao State University – Iligan Institute of Technology (MSU-IIT). Rooted in the spirit of excellence and unity, Langkit celebrates the vibrant culture, pride, and identity of the MSU-IIT community.

Our name, "Langkit", is inspired by the intricate Maranao woven patterns symbolizing creativity, heritage, and resilience—values that MSU-IIT proudly upholds. From apparel and accessories to stationery and collectibles, each item in our collection is thoughtfully designed to represent the true spirit of a true IITian.

Whether you're a student, alumnus, faculty member, or supporter, Langkit offers more than just merchandise—it’s a piece of home, a mark of belonging, and a statement of pride. Wear your colors. Carry your legacy. Live the Langkit.
